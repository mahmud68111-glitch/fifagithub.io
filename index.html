📋 Structure

Tournament Format

For the first time ever, 48 nations compete — a revolutionary expansion of the world's biggest event.

🏟️

Group Stage

12 groups of 4 teams. Top 2 from each group plus the 8 best 3rd-placed teams advance to the Round of 32.

⚔️

Round of 32

Brand new stage introduced in 2026. 32 teams battle in single-elimination knockout matches.

🏆

Knockout Rounds

Round of 16, Quarter-finals, Semi-finals — the classic elimination bracket leading to the Final.

🥇

The Final

The Grand Final — to be played at MetLife Stadium, New York on July 19, 2026.
